## Question 3  
**What are Active and Diffused Modes of Thinking?**

- **Focused (Active) Mode:**  
  This mode is used when we concentrate deeply on a task, like solving a coding problem. It involves logical and step-by-step thinking.

- **Diffused Mode:**  
  This mode works when we are relaxed, such as walking or resting. It helps in making broader connections and improves creativity and problem-solving ability.
